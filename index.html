About me

I am a ninth-cycle university student at the Pontifical Catholic University of Peru (PUCP), majoring in Sociology. I have work experience as a pre-professional intern in the Research area of the Instituto de Estudios Peruanos (IEP) for the design of a reintegration and educational promotion strategy funded by UNICEF.

Additionally, I have received awards such as the Scholarship for Academic Excellence (twice), the Academic Research Award, and the R.P. Felipe Mac Gregor, S.J. Academic Excellence Award. I have been a member of the Sociology student magazine at PUCP, 'La Colmena', and the PUCP Debate Society.

Recently, I received an scholarship from the Artificial Intelligence and Computational Methods Laboratory in Social Sciences (QLAB) - PUCP for the courses of Introduction to Python for Social Sciences, Machine Learning for Social Sciences, and Applied Econometrics (2023)

My main academic and professional insterests are related to computational social sciences, quantitative research methods and social theory.
